From snow-capped peaks to pine-covered trails, Himachal Pradesh invites you to discover its wild heart...

2. Top Mountain Ranges & Peaks in Himachal

  • Dhauladhar Range
  • Pir Panjal Range
  • Kinnaur Kailash
  • Hanuman Tibba
  • Friendship Peak

3. Best Treks in Himachal

  • Triund Trek – Easy-moderate, panoramic views of Dhauladhar
  • Hampta Pass Trek – Crossover trek with lush valleys and stark deserts
  • Pin Parvati Pass – Challenging and beautiful
  • Indrahar Pass – High-altitude adventure with glacier views
  • Bhrigu Lake Trek – Meadows and a high-altitude glacial lake

5. Cultural & Spiritual Aspects

Explore monasteries and local villages along the trekking routes. The spiritual vibe of Himachal's mountains is unmatched, offering peace and tranquility to all who visit.
