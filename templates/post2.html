Triund Trek

Difficulty: Easy

Best Time to Visit: March to June, September to December

Perfect for beginners, this trek near McLeod Ganj offers stunning views of the Dhauladhar range.

Hampta Pass

Difficulty: Moderate

Best Time to Visit: June to October

Experience crossing valleys and snowy landscapes on this picturesque trek.

Bhrigu Lake Trek

Difficulty: Moderate

Best Time to Visit: May to October

A scenic high-altitude lake trek near Manali, perfect for nature enthusiasts.

Pin Parvati Pass

Difficulty: Challenging

Best Time to Visit: July to September

For experienced trekkers, this trail offers a thrilling adventure through diverse terrains.
